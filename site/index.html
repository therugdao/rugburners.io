Rug Recovery Fund

The Rug Recovery Fund is a DAO designed to soothe the painful burns of holders. This may be the 9,999,999,999th DAO to exist in the NFT space, but it is the first one to work for its holders rather than the devs that present self-interested proposals week after week.

Regardless of whether the project sells 1% or 100%, the DAO will be funded with 16.67% of mint sales and 50% of all secondary sales. Each week, each holder (regardless of how many pieces held) will submit the name of a rugged project they were burned by and want swept. The top 5 projects with the most submissions will be eligible for that week’s sweep, and the holders will choose one of these 5 rugged projects by majority vote. 1 Burner = 1 vote. A holder with 10 Burners will have 10 votes. A Holder with 50 Burners will have 50 votes.

The project chosen by the DAO will become the “Rug of the Week”. The DAO will then vote on an eth amount from the Fund (up to 25% of the total fund for any given week). The Fund will use this eth to buy each piece holders have in the Rug of the Week at the initial mint price. You minted 10 Fish Tanks at .025 and the floor is at 0? Don’t worry. We’re buying all 10 of them from you at .025 each. If the eth amount chosen is insufficient to cover the holders’ bags in any given week, swept pieces will be randomly chosen during a live recording for complete transparency. If there is eth left over after any given sweep, it will be returned to the Fund. 

Each piece will be held in the Fund wallet and will be owned by the DAO. To the extent any of these pieces become valuable in the future, the DAO will vote whether to sell or hold with all proceeds going right back into the Fund. Additionally, once a rug is chosen by the DAO, it will undergo a month-long “Cooldown Period” where it cannot be chosen again. This Cooldown Period will help whale-proof Rug Burn and protect the smaller holders’ votes by ensuring larger holders cannot vote for the same project week after week.

We want to use the Launchpad to change the dynamic of the NFT world. Right now, there are a few greedy devs that one has to hire in order to bring their art and/or idea for an NFT project to life. These devs charge astronomical amounts of eth to create a smart contract and act as a barrier for entry.

The Launchpad will allow artists and dreamers to develop their own NFT collections without having to be extorted by these developers. All it takes is a few inputs and art uploads and VOILA! The Launchpad creates the smart contract for you.

ALL Rug Burn holders will have the opportunity to drop a project through the Launchpad. After Rug Burn mint, we will hold consultations with holders. We will provide every possible protection to make sure that the Launchpad is rug-proof. You will be able to mint projects worry-free. 5% of profits from ALL Launchpad projects will go back into the Rug Burn ecosystem.
